CAPABILITIES

The full edit, inside Premiere.

Eight capabilities at launch. The hero workflow is Story Cutter for documentary and drama editors. Everything else lives one click away in the same panel.

01 MVP

Story Cutter

Drop in a Trint, Otter, Descript, Rev, SRT, VTT or native Premiere transcript. The agent reads it, finds beats, builds a paper edit, and inserts the rough cut at the playhead. Multicam aware (stacked V1/V2/V3).

Color Grade Assistant

Frame-at-playhead capture, technical score 0–100, one-click "Apply Recommended Look" writes directly to the selected clip's Lumetri. Generate a custom .cube LUT. Skin-tone protection on.

Voiceover at Playhead
