## What it is

A native **Adobe Premiere Pro 25.6+ UXP plugin** that puts an AI editing companion directly in the editor's panel. Eight capabilities at launch, designed for the cohort the existing "AI for Premiere" tools cannot reach: film, documentary, drama, and podcast editors who already use Trint, Otter, Descript, or Rev.

## At launch (MVP)

- **Story Cutter** — beat extraction from Trint, Otter, Descript, Rev, SRT, VTT or native Premiere transcripts. Multicam aware. Insert rough cut at playhead.
- **Color Grade Assistant** — one-click Lumetri write + custom .cube LUT. Frame-at-playhead capture. Skin-tone protection.
- **Voiceover at Playhead** — AI audio voiceover directly into the timeline. Ported from CineSmith.
- **Image & Thumbnail Generation** — multi-model (Flux 2 Max, Nano Banana Pro, GPT Image 2, Seedream, Grok Imagine) with live cost preview.

## Why this exists

The existing AI plugin in this space ([ChatVideoPro](https://chatvideopro.com)) is built on Adobe's CEP runtime, which Adobe is removing by **November 2026**. It also accepts only Premiere's native `.json` transcript format — locking out editors who use Trint, Otter, Descript, or Rev (i.e., most working documentary and drama editors). It has zero audio integrations. CineSmithPro is the UXP-native, transcript-flexible, audio-capable alternative.
